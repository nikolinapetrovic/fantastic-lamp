My experience in Greece!

Lefkada is an exotic island! It has the very very best beaches i have ever seen in my life,And there are so many:porto katsiki, egremnoi(the best tin greece and surely in europe), kathisma etc.The colour of the water ,the sun, the sandy beach make you feel like you are in paradise There is not a greek island or any other Mediterranean spot where you can find such a beautiful beaches.

Lefkada’s turquoise waters have led to its nickname — the Caribbean of Greece — and images will speak for themselves.
